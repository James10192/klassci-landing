This linear path takes you from a brand-new tenant to a system ready to enroll students. **One hour**, 8 steps, a checkpoint at every milestone. By the end you'll know exactly where your setup stands.

For vocabulary and concepts, keep [Core concepts](/en/docs/concepts) open in another tab and check it whenever a term blocks you.

## Before you start

You'll need:

- your **tenant URL** (`https://your-school.klassci.com`)
- your **super-administrator credentials** (received by email)
- the list of your **filières** and **study levels**
- the **tuition-fee grid** (or at least the enrollment fees)
- the **email addresses** of your administrative and teaching staff
- **60 uninterrupted minutes**

<Callout>
  **Everything is editable later.** You can perfectly start with one filière and one fee, then enrich week by week. The goal here is reaching first enrollment, not perfection.
</Callout>

## Step 1 — Create the academic year (5 min)

Sidebar → **Filières & Classes → Années universitaires** → **+ Nouvelle année universitaire** button.

1. Fill in **Nom** (`2025-2026`), **Date de rentrée**, and **Date de fin**.
2. Leave **Année universitaire active** on. Click **Enregistrer**.
3. Back on the list, click the **yellow circle with a check** on the year's row to mark it as current.

![Academic year 2025-2026 marked as current with the green ANNÉE EN COURS badge](/img/docs/superadmin/onboarding/05-annee-courante.png)

<Callout>
  <Icon name="check" /> **Checkpoint** — a green **ANNÉE EN COURS** badge shows up on the row. Every future enrollment is tied to this year by default.
</Callout>

[Full walkthrough, screenshots and edge cases →](/en/docs/superadmin/onboarding#étape-1--créer-lannée-universitaire-courante)

## Step 2 — Create the filières (10 min)

Sidebar → **Filières & Classes → Filières** → **+ Nouvelle Filière** button.

For each filière in your offer:

1. **Nom** (`BATIMENT`, `INFORMATIQUE`, `Communication & Marketing`…) and short **Code** (`BTP`, `INFO`, `BH123`).
2. Leave **Filière active** on.
3. Click **Créer la filière**.

![Filière management page with several filières and their ACTIVE badge](/img/docs/superadmin/onboarding/06-filieres-liste.png)

<Callout>
  <Icon name="check" /> **Checkpoint** — every filière shows up with a green **ACTIVE** badge and the levels/courses counter at `0`.
</Callout>

If a filière starts with a **tronc commun** before specialisation, see the dedicated subsection in the full guide.

[Full walkthrough →](/en/docs/superadmin/onboarding#step-2-configure-your-filières)

## Step 3 — Create the study levels (5 min)

Sidebar → **Filières & Classes → Niveaux d'études** → **+ Nouveau Niveau** button.

For each level (BTS1, BTS2, L1, L2, M1…):

1. **Nom** (`BTS Première Année`), **Code** (`BTS1`).
2. **Type de formation** (`BTS`, `Licence`, `Master`, `Doctorat`) — this is what determines whether the level follows the BTS system or LMD.
3. **Année d'étude** (1st, 2nd, etc.) → **Créer le niveau**.

![Study levels page with four configured levels](/img/docs/superadmin/onboarding/08-niveaux-liste.png)

<Callout>
  <Icon name="check" /> **Checkpoint** — the levels appear in the table with their codes and the **ACTIF** badge.
</Callout>

[Full walkthrough →](/en/docs/superadmin/onboarding#step-3-create-the-study-levels)

## Step 4 — Create the classes (10 min)

Sidebar → **Filières & Classes → Classes** → **+ Nouvelle classe** button.

For every `filière × niveau` combination that will host students at the start of the year:

1. **Nom de la classe** (`1ère année BTS Génie Civil`), **Code** (`1BTS-GC-BAT`).
2. Pick **Filière**, **Niveau d'études**, and **Année universitaire**.
3. Set the **Capacité maximale** (default 30) → **Enregistrer la classe**.

![Class management page with the card grid](/img/docs/superadmin/onboarding/11-classes-liste.png)

<Callout>
  <Icon name="check" /> **Checkpoint** — every class shows up as a card with its **0 inscrits** counter and the **ACTIVE** badge.
</Callout>

The BTS/LMD academic system is auto-derived from the level (Licence/Master/Doctorat → LMD; otherwise BTS).

[Full walkthrough →](/en/docs/superadmin/onboarding#step-4-create-the-classes)

## Step 5 — Configure the courses (10 min)

### For BTS levels

Sidebar → **ENSEIGNEMENT → Matières** → **+ Nouvelle matière** button.

1. **Nom complet** (`Mathématiques appliquées`), **Type de formation** (`Formation générale` by default).
2. In the **Liaisons Filières & Niveaux** column on the right, **tick each level** where the course is taught. The **Tout sélectionner** button on a card ticks every level of that filière at once.
3. Click **Enregistrer la matière**.

![Course catalogue page with stats and the list of courses with their links](/img/docs/superadmin/onboarding/15-matieres-liste.png)

### For LMD levels

Sidebar → **SYSTÈME LMD → Unités d'Enseignement** → **+ Nouvelle UE** (fill name, code, ECTS credits, type) → on the UE row, click **<Icon name="plus-circle" className="text-emerald-500" />** to add each ECUE → then click **<Icon name="link" className="text-blue-500" />** to attach the UE to a parcours.

<Callout>
  <Icon name="check" /> **Checkpoint** — courses (BTS) or UE+ECUE (LMD) appear with their attached-combinations counter.
</Callout>

[BTS walkthrough →](/en/docs/superadmin/onboarding#step-5-configure-the-courses) · [LMD UE/ECUE →](/en/docs/superadmin/onboarding#54--lmd-system-structuring-with-ue-and-ecue)

## Step 6 — Configure fees (10 min)

Sidebar → **GESTION FINANCIÈRE → Comptabilité → Gestion des Frais** → **+ Nouvelle catégorie** button.

Create at least **two mandatory categories**:

1. **Frais d'inscription** (code `INSCRIPTION`, default amount, **Frais obligatoire** ticked).
2. **Frais de scolarité** (code `SCOLARITE`, default amount, mandatory).

Then **Configuration par Classe** in the header → click **Configurer les Frais** on each unconfigured class → enter the **3 MESRS amounts** (AFFECTÉS / RÉAFFECTÉS / NON AFFECTÉS).

![Per-class fee configuration page with cards showing Complet or Non configuré](/img/docs/superadmin/onboarding/28-config-par-classe.png)

<Callout>
  <Icon name="check" /> **Checkpoint** — every class card shows its green **Complet** badge once the 3 amounts are filled in for every mandatory fee. If a class isn't configured, the category's **default amount** is used as a fallback.
</Callout>

[Fees & Accounting module — full reference →](/en/docs/modules/frais-comptabilite)

## Step 7 — Create staff accounts (5 min)

Sidebar → **Personnel → Vue unifiée** → **+ Nouveau personnel** button.

For each staff member:

1. Fill in last name, first name, email.
2. Pick the **role** (`secretaire`, `enseignant`, `comptable`, `caissier`, `coordinateur`, `service_technique`).
3. Submit. KLASSCI generates a **username** in `firstname.lastname` format and a **secure initial password**, then sends an activation email.

<Callout>
  <Icon name="check" /> **Checkpoint** — the staff member shows up in the list with their role. On first sign-in they're forced to change their password.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warn">
  **Watch faculty permissions.** If you turn on the `notes_evaluations`, `presences`, and `emploi_temps` modules, also grant them to the `enseignant` role. The module toggle isn't enough — the business permission must be granted too.
</Callout>

[Full walkthrough →](/en/docs/superadmin/onboarding#step-7-create-staff-accounts)

## Step 8 — Validate and launch (5 min)

Before sharing the URL with students, run an **end-to-end test**:

1. **Étudiants → Nouvel étudiant** → create a test student, enroll them in a class.
2. Open their **financial standing**: the class's mandatory fees should be ready to charge.
3. Sign in with a **test faculty account** and verify grade entry works in their class.
4. Check the sidebar shows every expected module for each role.

<Callout>
  <Icon name="check" /> **Final checkpoint** — student created, fees visible, grades editable. You're ready to share the URL and credentials with your administrative team.
</Callout>
