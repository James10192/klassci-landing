---
title: Registrar — creating a new enrollment
description: From form to final validation — the full path that turns a prospect into an active KLASSCI student.
---

A KLASSCI enrollment moves through three phases:

1. **Capture** the enrollment record (student, class, MESRS status, parents, fees).
2. **Validate** it (with or without a payment) to activate the student account.
3. **Issue** the official documents (proof of enrollment, attendance certificate).

This page walks through the three phases in the order you meet them on screen.

## Where to start

In the sidebar, expand **Students** and click **New Enrollment**.

![Students sidebar with New Enrollment selected](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/01-sidebar-nouvelle-inscription.png)

The screen opens onto a five-section form. The numbered rail (1 to 5) on the right shows your progress through the form.

## 1 — Personal information

Civil identity of the student: surname, given name(s), gender, date and place of birth, nationality, phone, city, district.

![Form — personal information with manual matricule field](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/02-form-info-personnelles.png)

**Email** and **photo** are optional. The email is used to deliver login credentials once the enrollment is validated — if you leave it blank, KLASSCI shows the credentials in a printable modal at validation time.

<Callout>
  **Matricule.** Entered manually. Before saving, click **<Icon name="eye" /> Verify**: KLASSCI checks that this matricule does not already exist in the tenant. If your school uses a dedicated convention (for example `MESBTP25-0001`), keep to it — it makes exports and accounting imports much smoother.
</Callout>

## 2 — Academic information (class selection)

The full academic assignment flows from the class. The track, study level, and academic year are inferred automatically.

![Academic Information section with Select button](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/03-bouton-selectionner-classe.png)

Click the blue **<Icon name="plus" /> Select** button. A modal lists every class open for the current academic year.

![Class selection modal with filters and seat counts](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/04-modal-selection-classe.png)

Three columns help you choose: **CLASS**, **TRACK**, **LEVEL**. The **SEATS** column shows the enrolled / capacity ratio with a colour cue:

- **Green** — plenty of seats left
- **Orange** — capacity nearly reached
- **Red** — class full

Filter by track (top-left combo) or search by class / track / level name (top-right field). Click **Select** on the right row — the modal closes and the class is bound to the enrollment.

The **Academic year** field is pre-filled with the tenant's current year. You can change it for retroactive or anticipated enrollments.

## 3 — MESRS enrollment status

The government enrollment status decides which amounts get billed.

![Government enrollment status section with Affecté / Réaffecté / Non affecté combo](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/05-statut-affectation-mesrs.png)

Three options:

- **Affecté** — the student was assigned to this track by MESRS. The State covers part of the fees; the student only pays the configured student share.
- **Réaffecté** — the student was redirected by MESRS from another track. Same or close pricing as Affecté, depending on your matrix.
- **Non affecté** — private student, no MESRS coverage. Pays the full private rate.

The right-hand panel summarises the financial impact as soon as you pick. For the full matrix, see [Fees & accounting — MESRS matrix](/en/docs/modules/frais-comptabilite#matrice-mesrs).

## 4 — Parents / Guardians (optional)

Collapsible section. An enrollment can have zero, one, or several parents/guardians attached.

![Parents/Guardians section expanded with one parent and Add button](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/06-parents-tuteurs-optionnel.png)

Per parent: **surname**, **given name(s)**, **phone** (all three become required if the section is filled), then email, profession, relationship (Father / Mother / Guardian / Other), and address are optional.

<Callout>
  **Pick an existing parent.** If the parent is already in KLASSCI for a sibling, tick **<Icon name="eye" /> Select an existing parent** at the top of the parent card. Search by name or phone — KLASSCI links the existing parent without creating a duplicate contact.
</Callout>

The **+ Add another parent / guardian** button opens a second identical card. The red **<Icon name="x" /> Remove** button drops a parent.

<Callout type="warn">
  **No separate « parent » role in KLASSCI.** In Côte d'Ivoire, parents in practice use their child's account to follow the schooling. Parental information is kept for contact purposes (reminders, emergencies) but does not create a user account.
</Callout>

## 5 — Enrollment fees and options

This section becomes active as soon as a class is selected.

### Mandatory fees

Auto-checked based on class, track and MESRS status. The amount used is:

1. The **MESRS variant** configured for this class (Affecté / Réaffecté / Non affecté), if it exists.
2. Otherwise, the **default amount** of the fee category (entered when the category was created).

![Mandatory fees pre-checked with default amounts](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/07-frais-obligatoires-auto.png)

The orange **Configuration incomplete** banner shows up when a MESRS variant is missing for this class. The **default amount** is applied — the enrollment is never blocked. The **<Icon name="settings" /> Quick configuration** link jumps you straight into the fee grid to fill the gap.

### Optional fees

Not checked by default. The student (or parent) explicitly opts in.

![CANTINE optional fee with formula choice](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/08-formule-cantine.png)

Click **<Icon name="plus-circle" /> Subscribe** on the row. For multi-formula services (canteen with *Full meal*, *Main course*, etc.), pick the formula — the running total updates automatically.

![Green Subscribed chip with red cross to cancel](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/09-frais-souscrit-chip.png)

The green **<Icon name="check" /> Subscribed** chip confirms it. The **red <Icon name="x" /> cross** cancels the subscription before saving.

The **selected fees summary** at the bottom of the section keeps the running total in sight.

Finally click **<Icon name="save" /> Save enrollment**.

## The enrollment record

The enrollment is created but **not yet validated**. Status shown: **Pending** / step **Prospect**.

![Enrollment record — blue header with name, matricule, Pending and Prospect badges](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/10-fiche-inscription-header.png)

Three main zones make up the record.

### Identity and academic

The left column repeats the civil information. The right column summarises the academic assignment: track, level, class, academic year, MESRS status, enrollment date, type (First enrollment / Re-enrollment).

### Financial situation

Three KPIs: **Total expected** / **Total paid** (validated only) / **Remaining balance**, with a progress bar.

![Detail by fee with Tuition fees and Enrollment fees rows](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/11-fiche-inscription-finances.png)

The **Detail by fee** table lists each category with its type (Mandatory / Optional), expected amount, paid amount, balance, and settlement status. The **Pay** button on each row opens the payment modal directly scoped to that category.

### Available optional fees & linked payments

![Available optional fees + Linked payments (empty)](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/12-fiche-paiements-vide.png)

The **Available Optional Fees** zone lists services that were not subscribed at creation — you can still subscribe the student here (handy when the decision is made after the enrollment interview). Click **+ Subscribe** or **<Icon name="plus-circle" /> Subscribe student** on the card.

The **Payments linked to this enrollment** zone is empty until at least one payment has been associated.

## Action bar

The blue bar at the top of the record holds the main actions.

![Action bar: Administration, Validate with payment, Validate, Edit, Student record, Back](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/13-bandeau-actions.png)

| Button | Effect |
|---|---|
| **<Icon name="settings" /> Administration** | Advanced settings (cancellation, transfer to another class) |
| **<Icon name="save" /> Validate with payment** | Recommended shortcut: associate a first payment and validate in one step |
| **<Icon name="check" /> Validate** | Direct validation with no payment (pending scholarship, deferred payment) |
| **<Icon name="pencil" /> Edit** | Back to the form — track, level, class remain editable until the enrollment is finally validated |
| **<Icon name="eye" /> Student record** | Preview of the student record (largely empty until validation is done) |
| **Back** | Enrollments list |

<Callout type="warn">
  **Why validation is mandatory.** As long as an enrollment stays **Prospect / Pending**, the student has **no KLASSCI account**: not in class lists, cannot be graded, no credentials sent. Validation is the step that turns the application into an active presence in the system.
</Callout>

## Validate the enrollment

Two routes depending on whether the first payment is already in or not.

### Path A — Validate with payment (recommended shortcut)

Click **<Icon name="save" /> Validate with payment**. The payment association modal opens.

![Associate a payment to the enrollment modal, empty](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/14-modal-paiement-vide.png)

Required fields:

- **Amount paid** (FCFA) — partial payments are fine.
- **Fee category** — combo showing the mandatory fees on this enrollment. Once a category is picked, KLASSCI shows the **Maximum allowed amount** so you do not over-collect by accident.
- **Payment method** — Cash, Wave, Orange Money, MTN MoMo, Cheque, Bank transfer.
- **Payment date** — pre-filled with today.
- **Payment reference** *(optional)* — cheque number, Wave / OM / MoMo transaction ID, transfer reference.
- **Notes** *(optional)* — free comment kept in the history.

![Modal filled with 50,000 FCFA and Cash method](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/22-modal-paiement-rempli.png)

The **<Icon name="check" /> Validate the payment immediately** checkbox:

- **Checked** — the payment is recorded as validated right away.
- **Unchecked** — the payment goes into *Pending validation*. Accounting must validate it manually from the *Payments to validate* tab.

Click **<Icon name="save" /> Associate the payment**. A confirmation toast shows up and the action bar now exposes a solid green **<Icon name="check" /> Validate** button.

![Toast Payment associated successfully and green Validate button](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/23-toast-paiement-bouton-valider.png)

### Path B — Validate without payment

Click **<Icon name="check" /> Validate** straight from the action bar, skipping the payment step.

Useful for:

- Scholarship students (state payment expected later).
- Deferred enrollments (first payment next month).
- Schools that validate at admission and collect at the start of term.

The enrollment becomes *Active* with an outstanding balance. The student account is still created: the student can see their financial record and what is left to pay.

## Final validation

Whether you take path A or B, the **Validate** button opens a confirmation modal.

![Final validation modal with the list of frozen fields](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/24-modal-validation-definitive.png)

KLASSCI lists the items that will be **frozen** after validation:

- **Track**
- **Study level**
- **Class**

A **free-form note** can be added — it stays visible in the enrollment history.

Confirm with **<Icon name="check" /> Validate definitively**. Two things happen at once:

1. The enrollment **status** flips to **Active**.
2. The student's **user account** is generated (credentials sent by email if filled in, otherwise shown in a printable modal).

![Toast Prospect converted to student successfully, Active badge](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/25-toast-converti-actif.png)

![Active status with partial financial situation (50,000 of 250,000 settled)](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/26-statut-actif-final.png)

<Callout>
  **Correcting after validation.** If the class needs to change after validation (re-orientation, data-entry mistake), go through **<Icon name="settings" /> Administration → Transfer to another class** from the enrollment record. A trail is kept and fees are recomputed for the new class.
</Callout>

## The student record

Once the enrollment is validated, open the full student record from the **<Icon name="eye" /> Student record** button or via **Students → Student list**.

![Student record — blue header with name, class, badges, and counters](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/15-fiche-etudiant-header.png)

The blue header shows:

- **Identity** (full name, class, track, level, current year).
- **Badges**: system (LMD / BTS), matricule, enrollment status, nationality.
- **Counters**: Enrollments / Attendance / Absences / Paid (FCFA).
- **Actions**: Edit / Documents / Back.

Six tabs organise the rest.

### Overview

Four key KPIs (average grade, attendance rate, ECTS credits, % settled payments), then a *Quick info* card and the year-by-year list of enrollments.

![Overview tab — quick info + 2025-2026 enrollment card](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/16-fiche-etudiant-informations-rapides.png)

When the enrollment has not been finally validated yet, a **yellow callout** reminds the action and offers a **<Icon name="check" /> Validate enrollment** shortcut button.

### Academic

Yearly summary: overall average, ranking, mention, and grades by subject as soon as they are entered.

![Academic tab — empty summary at the start of the year](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/17-onglet-academique.png)

### Attendance

Overall attendance rate and the absence / lateness history (filled by the *Attendance* module).

### Finances

Detailed financial KPIs, **Detail by fee**, and a **<Icon name="plus-circle" /> Record a payment** button to capture a new payment any time.

![Finances tab with Total expected / Paid / Remaining and Pay buttons per row](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/18-onglet-finances.png)

The **<Icon name="file-text" /> Financial situation** (toast) and **<Icon name="file-text" /> PDF Statement** (download) buttons let you produce the family-facing statement.

### Profile

Full personal information and a **User account** zone: username, login email, last login, account creation date, and a **<Icon name="settings" /> Reset password** button (sends a fresh temporary password by email or shows it in a modal).

![Profile tab with Active User account section](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/19-onglet-profil.png)

### Documents

File attachments on the file (received certificates, ID copies, admission folders). The red badge on the tab signals newly added documents.

![Documents tab with one proof of enrollment uploaded](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/20-onglet-documents.png)

**<Icon name="plus" /> Add a document** lets you attach any file to the record.

## Issuing a proof of enrollment or attendance certificate

Two official documents are available as soon as an enrollment is validated. Click the **<Icon name="file-text" /> Documents** button in the student record header.

![Documents dropdown — Proof of enrollment and Attendance certificate, each with Preview and Download PDF](/img/docs/secretaire/inscription/21-dropdown-documents-pdf.png)

| Document | When to issue |
|---|---|
| **Proof of enrollment** | Official paperwork (bank, embassy, transport pass, visa file) — confirms the student is enrolled for the current year |
| **Attendance certificate** | Justify past actual presence (employer, family allowance, internship) — covers a previous period |

For each document: **<Icon name="eye" /> Preview** opens the PDF in a new tab, **<Icon name="file-text" /> Download PDF** saves it directly.

<Callout>
  **Custom document.** For anything else attached to the file (scanned birth certificate, ID copy, scholarship justification, recommendation letter), go through the student record's **Documents** tab and click **<Icon name="plus" /> Add a document**. The file is bound to the student, not to a specific enrollment — it stays accessible across years.
</Callout>

## Edge cases

### Re-enrollment for the next year

Do **not** create a brand-new enrollment for an existing student. Use **Students → Re-enrollments**: KLASSCI carries the profile and the parents over and only asks for the new class and the initial payment. The matricule stays the same.

### « Conditional » enrollment

If the file is incomplete (missing document, study fee not yet paid), save the enrollment without validating and place it under **Conditional** (dedicated sidebar entry under Students). It will leave that list when validated definitively.

### Cancellation before validation

**<Icon name="settings" /> Administration → Cancel enrollment** from the record. No fee is billed, the matricule is freed, the file leaves the active listings (kept in archive).

### Cancellation after validation

Possible but the history is kept: the student account is deactivated, fees already paid stay visible in accounting (refunds are your call). See **<Icon name="settings" /> Administration → Cancel enrollment** on the record.

### Optional fees subscribed after validation

Possible at any time from the enrollment record (**Available Optional Fees** zone) or from the student record's **Finances** tab. The amount is added to the remaining balance.
